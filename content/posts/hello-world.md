I've wanted to start a tech blog for years. The idea has been floating around in my head for a long time, but I never found the right combination of time, energy, and motivation to actually sit down and do it.

What changed is that I've been building a lot of things lately (tools, libraries, and projects that I'm genuinely excited about), and I realized I want to start writing about them, not just to document what I've built, but to share the ideas, trade-offs, and lessons learned along the way.

So, here it is! The next post will be about [Mythic](https://github.com/joshburgess/mythic), the static site generator I built to power this very blog.
